## Upgrade Playbooks

### Upgrade Path: Standard ($99/month) → Premium ($199/month)

**Value Proposition:**
- Advanced analytics & reporting
- Priority support (2-hour response vs. 4-hour)
- Custom integrations (beyond standard)
- Dedicated account manager
- Advanced training (quarterly optimization sessions)

**Upgrade Triggers:**

**Trigger 1: High Usage (90+ days as customer)**
- Metrics: 100+ calls/month, 50+ appointments/month
- Message: "You're a power user! Premium gives you advanced analytics to optimize even further."
- Close rate: 35%

**Trigger 2: Multiple Support Requests**
- Metrics: 3+ support tickets in 30 days
- Message: "Premium includes priority support (2-hour response) and dedicated account manager."
- Close rate: 40%

**Trigger 3: Requesting Advanced Features**
- Metrics: Customer asks about features in Premium tier
- Message: "That feature is included in Premium. Want to see a demo?"
- Close rate: 50%

**Trigger 4: 6+ Months as Standard Customer**
- Metrics: Proven value, established relationship
- Message: "You've been with us 6 months. Ready for Premium features?"
- Close rate: 30%

---

### Upgrade Playbook: High Usage Trigger

**Step 1: Identify Opportunity (Automated)**
- Health scoring system flags: Usage score >90, 90+ days as customer
- Alert sent to customer success manager

**Step 2: Preparation (Day 1)**
- Review customer metrics (usage, value, satisfaction)
- Prepare personalized ROI calculation
- Schedule upgrade call (15-30 minutes)

**Step 3: Outreach (Day 1-2)**
```
Subject: Unlock Advanced Analytics - Premium Upgrade Available

Hi [Name],

I noticed you're doing amazing with Cobalt AI:
- [X] calls handled this month
- [Y] appointments booked (87% conversion!)
- $[Z] in after-hours revenue captured

You're in the top 10% of our customers by usage. Premium tier gives you:

1. Advanced Analytics
   - Predictive insights (conversion trends, revenue forecasting)
   - Custom reports (export to Excel, scheduled reports)
   - Performance benchmarks (how you compare to industry)

2. Priority Support
   - 2-hour response time (vs. 4-hour Standard)
   - Dedicated account manager
   - Proactive optimization calls (quarterly)

3. Custom Integrations
   - Beyond standard integrations (your custom CRM, tools)
   - API access (build your own integrations)

Want to see a quick demo? [Schedule 15-minute call]

Or upgrade now: [Upgrade Link]

Questions? Just reply to this email.

Best,
[Customer Success Manager]
```

**Step 4: Upgrade Call (Day 3-5)**
- Duration: 15-30 minutes
- Show Premium features (live demo)
- Calculate ROI: "Premium costs $100/month more, but advanced analytics will help you capture an extra $[amount]/month. That's a [X]x return."
- Handle objections
- Close: "Ready to upgrade? I can activate Premium right now."

**Step 5: Follow-Up (Day 7)**
- If upgraded: Welcome to Premium, schedule onboarding call
- If not: Check in, answer questions, reschedule if needed

**Close Rate Target:** 35%

## Multi-Location Expansion

### Expansion Playbook: Single Location → Multi-Location

**Value Proposition:**
- Unified dashboard (manage all locations in one place)
- Bulk pricing (20% discount for 3+ locations)
- Enterprise features (multi-location reporting, consolidated analytics)
- Centralized billing (one invoice, easier accounting)

**Expansion Triggers:**

**Trigger 1: Customer Mentions Other Locations**
- Detection: Support tickets, calls, emails mention "other location" or "we have multiple shops"
- Action: Immediate flag, customer success manager outreach
- Close rate: 60%

**Trigger 2: Success at First Location (6+ Months)**
- Detection: Proven ROI at first location, health score >80, 6+ months as customer
- Action: Proactive outreach offering expansion
- Close rate: 50%

**Trigger 3: Requesting Multi-Location Features**
- Detection: Customer asks about managing multiple locations
- Action: Offer multi-location expansion
- Close rate: 70%

---

### Expansion Playbook: Customer Mentions Other Locations

**Step 1: Identify Opportunity (Real-Time)**
- Support ticket/call/email mentions "other location" or "multiple shops"
- Alert sent to customer success manager immediately

**Step 2: Immediate Follow-Up (Same Day)**
```
Subject: Multi-Location Management - Expand Cobalt AI?

Hi [Name],

I noticed you mentioned [other location/multiple shops]. We can help you manage all your locations from one dashboard.

Benefits:
- Unified dashboard (see all locations at once)
- Bulk pricing (20% discount for 3+ locations)
- Consolidated reporting (compare performance across locations)
- Centralized billing (one invoice)

Deployment is fast: [Location 1] took 7 days. We can deploy to [Location 2] in the same timeframe.

Want to discuss? [Schedule 30-minute call]

Best,
[Customer Success Manager]
```

**Step 3: Expansion Call (Day 1-3)**
- Duration: 30 minutes
- Review success at Location 1 (ROI, metrics, satisfaction)
- Discuss Location 2 needs (similar or different?)
- Explain deployment process (7 days, same as Location 1)
- Present pricing (bulk discount: $99/month per location → $79/month for 3+ locations)
- Handle objections
- Close: "Ready to expand? I can start deployment tomorrow."

**Step 4: Deployment (Day 1-7)**
- Same 7-day deployment process as Location 1
- Leverage learnings from Location 1 (faster deployment)
- Customer success manager coordinates

**Step 5: Success Verification (Day 14)**
- Both locations operational
- Review metrics (both locations performing)
- Celebrate success
- Identify Location 3 opportunity (if applicable)

**Close Rate Target:** 60%

---

## Referral Programs

### Referral Program Structure

**For Referrers (Existing Customers):**
- Refer 1 customer: $100 credit (1 month free)
- Refer 3 customers: $500 credit (5 months free)
- Refer 5 customers: Lifetime 20% discount

**For Referees (New Customers):**
- 20% off first 3 months (referral discount)
- Priority onboarding (faster deployment)

**Track Referrals:**
- Unique referral link per customer
- Track: Who referred who, status, reward payout

---

### Referral Playbook: Happy Customers

**Step 1: Identify Referral Candidates (Automated)**
- Health scoring system flags: Health score >85, 3+ months as customer, high satisfaction (4.5+/5.0)
- Alert sent to customer success manager

**Step 2: Outreach (Day 1)**
```
Subject: Know Someone Who Could Use Cobalt AI?

Hi [Name],

You've been crushing it with Cobalt AI:
- 87% conversion rate
- $[amount]/year in after-hours revenue
- 4.8/5.0 satisfaction

Know another [auto shop/medical practice] that could benefit?

Referral rewards:
- Refer 1 customer: $100 credit (1 month free)
- Refer 3 customers: $500 credit (5 months free)
- Refer 5 customers: Lifetime 20% discount

Your referral link: [Unique Link]

They get 20% off first 3 months, too.

Thanks for being an amazing customer!

Best,
[Customer Success Manager]
```

**Step 3: Follow-Up (Monthly)**
- Remind happy customers about referral program
- Share success stories (other customers' results)
- Celebrate referrals (thank you message + credit)

**Referral Rate Target:** 20% of customers refer 1+ new customer

---

### Referral Program Automation

**Automated Triggers:**
- 3 months as customer + health score >85 → Send referral invitation
- 6 months as customer + health score >85 → Send referral reminder
- Referral successful → Send thank you + credit applied automatically
- Referral milestone reached (3, 5 referrals) → Send milestone reward
