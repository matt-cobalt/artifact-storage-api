**5. Success Stories**

- ROI achievements

- Video testimonials

**6. Vertical-Specific (Auto/Medical)**
- Automotive-specific discussions
- Medical-specific discussions
- Cross-vertical learning

**7. Off-Topic**
- General discussions
- Industry news
- Networking

---

### Forum Moderation

**Moderators:**
- 1-2 community managers (Cobalt AI team)
- 5-10 customer moderators (trusted community members)

**Moderation Guidelines:**
- Be respectful (no personal attacks)
- Stay on topic (move off-topic to appropriate category)
- No spam (promotional posts only in designated areas)
- Help others (encourage peer support)

**Moderation Actions:**
- Warning (first offense)
- Temporary ban (3-7 days for repeat offenses)
- Permanent ban (for severe violations)

---

### Forum Engagement Strategy

**Daily Engagement:**
- Community manager posts daily (tips, updates, questions)
- Respond to all questions within 24 hours
- Celebrate customer wins (acknowledge success stories)

**Weekly Engagement:**
- Weekly tips/feature spotlights
- "Question of the Week" (encourage discussion)
- Customer spotlight (feature one customer per week)

**Monthly Engagement:**
- Monthly community call (virtual meetup)
- Product roadmap update
- Community stats (engagement metrics)

## Ambassador Program

### Ambassador Criteria

**Requirements:**
- 6+ months as customer
- Health score >85
- Satisfaction score >4.5/5.0
- Active in community (10+ forum posts)
- Referred 2+ customers (or willing to)

**Ambassador Benefits:**
- Lifetime 30% discount (on subscription)
- Early access to new features (beta testing)
- Exclusive ambassador events (quarterly virtual meetups)
- Recognition (featured on website, case studies)
- Swag (t-shirts, stickers, etc.)
- Direct line to product team (influence roadmap)

---

### Ambassador Responsibilities

**Required (Minimum):**
- Refer 2+ customers per year
- 5+ forum posts per month (help others)
- 1 case study or testimonial per year
- Participate in quarterly ambassador calls

**Optional (Encouraged):**
- Host user group meetups
- Write blog posts or articles
- Speak at industry events
- Create video testimonials

---

### Ambassador Recruitment

**Month 1-3:**
- Identify top 20 customers (health score >85, 6+ months)
- Reach out personally (founder or customer success manager)
- Invite to ambassador program

**Month 4-6:**
- Expand to top 50 customers
- Launch ambassador application (self-nomination)
- Review applications monthly

**Month 7-12:**
- Open application to all customers (6+ months, health score >80)
- Accept 5-10 new ambassadors per month
- Maintain 25-50 active ambassadors

## Case Study Program

### Case Study Criteria

**Ideal Candidates:**
- 3+ months as customer
- Proven ROI (15,000%+ ROI, $50K+/year value)
- High satisfaction (4.5+/5.0)
- Willing to share metrics (conversion rate, revenue impact)
- Willing to be featured (name, company, photo)

---

### Case Study Process

**Step 1: Identify Candidate (Automated)**
- Health scoring system flags: Health score >85, proven ROI, 3+ months
- Customer success manager reaches out

**Step 2: Outreach (Day 1)**
```
Subject: Feature You in a Case Study?

Hi [Name],

You've been crushing it with Cobalt AI:
- 87% conversion rate (vs. 45% industry average)
- $[amount]/year in after-hours revenue captured
- 15,105% ROI validated

We'd love to feature you in a case study to help other [auto shops/medical practices] see what's possible.

What's involved:
- 30-minute interview (phone/video)
- Share your metrics (conversion rate, ROI, revenue impact)
- Optional: Video testimonial (5 minutes)
- Optional: Photos of your [shop/clinic]

What you get:
- Featured on our website (link to your business)
- Social media promotion (LinkedIn, Twitter)
- Recognition in our community
- Copy of case study (for your marketing)

Interested? [Schedule Interview Link]

Best,
[Customer Success Manager]
```

**Step 3: Interview (Day 3-7)**
- Duration: 30 minutes
- Questions: Background, problem, solution, results, advice
- Record (with permission) for video testimonial

**Step 4: Case Study Creation (Day 7-14)**
- Write case study (500-1,000 words)
- Include metrics, quotes, photos (if provided)
- Get customer approval (review before publishing)

**Step 5: Publication (Day 14-21)**
- Publish on website (case studies page)
- Share on social media (LinkedIn, Twitter)
- Email to community (newsletter)
- Use in sales (share with prospects)
