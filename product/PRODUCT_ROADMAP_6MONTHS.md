## Roadmap Overview

### North Star Metric

**Primary: Net Revenue Retention (NRR) - Target: 130%+**
- Measures: Expansion + retention combined (expansion revenue - churn)
- Why: Proves product-market fit for Series A
- Calculation: (Starting MRR + Expansion - Churn) / Starting MRR × 100
- Current: 110% (estimated, baseline)
- Target: 130%+ by Month 6

**Secondary Metrics:**
- Conversion rate: Maintain 87%+ (vs. 45-60% industry average)
- Customer satisfaction: Maintain 4.8/5+ (vs. 4.2/5 industry average)
- Time to value: <7 days (first appointment booked after deployment)
- Feature adoption: 60%+ of customers use 5+ features
- Support ticket deflection: 60%+ resolved via self-service

## Feature Specifications (Top 10)

### Feature Spec #1: Multi-Location Dashboard

**Problem Statement:**
Enterprise customers (franchises, PE firms, clinic groups) manage 5-50 locations. They need consolidated view across all locations, but current dashboard only shows 1 location at a time. Manual switching between locations is slow and doesn't enable portfolio-level insights.

**User Stories:**
- As a franchise owner, I want to see conversion rates across all 12 locations so I can identify underperformers
- As a PE firm analyst, I want to compare revenue metrics across 30 clinics so I can find optimization opportunities
- As a regional manager, I want to drill down from region → state → city → location so I can investigate issues
- As an executive, I want to see portfolio health at a glance so I can make strategic decisions

**Solution:**
Hierarchical dashboard with rollup metrics:

**Level 1: Portfolio Overview**
- Total locations: 12
- Aggregate conversion rate: 84.2% (weighted average)
- Total revenue impact: $788K/month
- Health score distribution: 9 healthy (80+), 2 at-risk (50-79), 1 critical (<50)
- Top performers: Top 3 locations by conversion rate
- Needs attention: Locations with health score <70

**Level 2: Location List (Sortable Table)**
| Location | Conv Rate | Revenue | Health | Trend | Actions |
|----------|-----------|---------|--------|-------|---------|
| Shop A   | 89.2%     | $67K    | ✅ 95  | ↗️ +2% | View    |
| Shop B   | 81.1%     | $54K    | ⚠️ 68  | ↘️ -5% | Fix     |
| Shop C   | 73.4%     | $42K    | 🔴 42  | ↘️ -12%| Urgent  |

**Level 3: Location Deep Dive**
- Click any location → full single-location dashboard
- Breadcrumb navigation: Portfolio > Region > Location
- Comparison view: Compare 2-5 locations side-by-side

**Technical Approach:**
```typescript
// Use materialized view aggregations (pre-computed for performance)
SELECT 
  portfolio_id,
  COUNT(DISTINCT location_id) as total_locations,
  AVG(conversion_rate) as avg_conversion,
  SUM(revenue) as total_revenue,
  AVG(health_score) as avg_health,
  COUNT(CASE WHEN health_score < 50 THEN 1 END) as critical_count,
  COUNT(CASE WHEN health_score BETWEEN 50 AND 79 THEN 1 END) as at_risk_count
FROM mv_daily_location_performance
WHERE portfolio_id = ? AND date = CURRENT_DATE
GROUP BY portfolio_id
```

**UI/UX Design:**
- Dashboard layout: Top-level summary cards, location list below
- Filtering: By region, health status, performance tier
- Sorting: By conversion rate, revenue, health score, trend
- Actions: View details, optimize, contact support

**Success Metrics:**
- 100% of enterprise customers use multi-location dashboard
- Time to identify issues: <2 minutes (vs 20+ minutes manual)
- Enterprise deal conversion: 40% → 70% (dashboard as differentiator)

**Engineering Estimate:** 2 weeks
- Backend: 1 week (aggregation queries, API endpoints)
- Frontend: 1 week (dashboard UI, location list, drill-down)

**Priority:** P0 (blocks enterprise sales)

## Prioritization Framework

**Criteria:**
- Blocks launch, enterprise sales, or compliance
- Required for legal/regulatory compliance
- Blocks >$100K revenue pipeline
- Unblocks team productivity (critical path)

**Examples:**
- Multi-location dashboard (blocks enterprise sales)
- SOC2 certification (required for enterprise)
- Public API v1 (platform strategy)
- Performance at scale (technical foundation)

**Timeline:** Ship ASAP (1-3 weeks)

**Approval:** Founder approval required

---

### P1 - Should Have (High Impact)

**Criteria:**
- Significant revenue, retention, or competitive impact
- 10%+ improvement in key metric
- Strategic differentiation
- High customer demand (top 5 requests)

**Examples:**
- Integration marketplace (growth accelerator)
- Auto-optimization engine (competitive moat)
- HVAC vertical launch (strategic validation)
- Predictive intelligence (Series A story)

**Timeline:** Ship within quarter (4-8 weeks)

**Approval:** Engineering lead + founder approval

---

### P2 - Nice to Have (Strategic Optionality)

**Criteria:**
- Future opportunities, experimentation, learning
- Moderate impact (5-10% improvement)
- Nice-to-have features (not blockers)
- Strategic optionality (opens new markets)

**Examples:**
- Voice customization studio (monetization)
- White-label solution (channel sales)
- Agent marketplace (ecosystem expansion)
