## Contingency Plans

### If Deployment Falls Behind

- Extend deployment to 10 days (still within Week 2)
- Add manual deployment support
- Prioritize highest-value customers

---

### If System Performance Degrades

- Activate PHOENIX recovery (rollback + stabilize)
- Reduce deployment velocity
- Scale infrastructure (add capacity)

---

### If Customer Issues Spike

- All-hands customer support
- Pause new deployments until stable
- Deploy emergency fixes

---

### If Major Incident

- Follow incident response playbook
- Communicate transparently with customers
- Fix, document, prevent recurrence
