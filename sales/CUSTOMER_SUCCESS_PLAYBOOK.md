## Customer Success Overview

### Mission

**Ensure every customer achieves their ROI targets and becomes a long-term partner.**

**Key Objectives:**
1. **Retention:** Maintain <5% annual churn (best-in-class SaaS)
2. **Expansion:** 20%+ upsell rate (Standard → Premium)

4. **Advocacy:** 20%+ referral rate (customers refer others)

## Onboarding Process

### Phase 1: Welcome (Day 0)

**Goals:**
- Welcome customer
- Set expectations
- Collect information
- Schedule kickoff

**Activities:**
- [ ] Send welcome email (immediately after signup)
- [ ] Schedule kickoff call (Day 1-2)
- [ ] Send onboarding checklist (what to expect)
- [ ] Collect integration credentials (Tekmetric, EHR, phone)

**Welcome Email Template:**
```
Subject: Welcome to Cobalt AI - Let's Get You Set Up

Hi [Name],

Welcome to Cobalt AI! We're excited to help [Shop Name] capture $[amount]/year in after-hours revenue.

Next Steps:
1. Schedule kickoff call (link to calendar)
2. Review onboarding checklist (attached)
3. Prepare integration credentials (we'll need these)

We'll be in touch within 24 hours to schedule your kickoff call.

Looking forward to working with you!

[Your Name]
Customer Success Manager
```

### Phase 2: Kickoff Call (Day 1-2)

**Duration:** 60 minutes

**Agenda:**
1. Introductions (5 minutes)
2. Review onboarding timeline (10 minutes)
3. Collect credentials and setup info (15 minutes)

**Key Questions:**
- "What's your biggest pain point we should solve first?"

**Outcomes:**

- Integration credentials collected

- Communication preferences established

### Common Support Issues & Solutions

**Issue 1: "OTTO isn't answering calls"**

**Diagnosis:**
- Check phone integration status (dashboard)
- Check Twilio logs (error messages)
- Verify phone number porting (completed?)

**Solution:**
- Fix integration (if broken)
- Complete phone porting (if pending)
- Test routing (make test call)
- Verify in dashboard

**Response Time:** <4 hours (Standard), <2 hours (Premium), <1 hour (Enterprise)

---

**Issue 2: "Conversion rate is lower than expected"**

**Diagnosis:**
- Review call recordings (listen to recent calls)
- Check conversion metrics (dashboard)
- Compare to historical (before Cobalt AI)
- Identify patterns (what's not converting?)

**Solution:**
- Optimize OTTO prompts (if needed)
- Adjust qualification questions (if needed)
- Review pricing (CAL agent accuracy)
- Provide training (staff on best practices)

**Response Time:** <24 hours (investigation), <48 hours (solution implemented)

---

**Issue 3: "Integration data is wrong"**

**Diagnosis:**
- Check data sync status (dashboard)
- Compare Tekmetric/EHR data vs dashboard
- Identify discrepancies
- Check last sync time

**Solution:**
- Fix data sync (refresh, re-sync)
- Update credentials (if expired)
- Fix source data (if Tekmetric/EHR issue)
- Verify fix (test sync)

**Response Time:** <4 hours (Standard), <2 hours (Premium)

**Triggers:**
- High usage (lots of calls, high volume)

- Requesting advanced features (analytics, priority support)

## Retention Strategies

### Churn Prevention

**Early Warning Signs:**
- Decreased usage (fewer calls, logins)
- Support ticket spike (frustration)
- Payment issues (failed payments)
- Negative feedback (surveys, support)

**Intervention:**
1. Identify at-risk customer (health score <50)
2. Reach out immediately (email, phone)
3. Understand issue ("What's not working?")
4. Fix quickly (<48 hours)
5. Follow up ("Is it fixed? Are you happy?")

## Customer Health Monitoring

### Health Score Calculation

**Formula:**
```
Health Score = (Usage Score × 0.4) + (Value Score × 0.3) + (Engagement Score × 0.2) + (Risk Score × 0.1)
```

**Components:**

**Usage Score (0-100):**
- Login frequency (daily = 100, weekly = 80, monthly = 60, never = 0)
- Feature adoption (using 80%+ features = 100, 50-80% = 75, <50% = 50)
- Call volume (high = 100, medium = 75, low = 50, none = 0)

**Value Score (0-100):**
- ROI achieved (above target = 100, at target = 80, below target = 60)
- Revenue impact (high = 100, medium = 75, low = 50)
- Time saved (high = 100, medium = 75, low = 50)

**Engagement Score (0-100):**
- Support interactions (engaged = 100, moderate = 75, low = 50)
- Training attendance (all sessions = 100, some = 75, none = 50)
- Feedback provided (positive = 100, neutral = 75, negative = 50)

**Risk Score (0-100, inverted - lower risk = higher score):**
- Support tickets (none = 100, few = 80, many = 60, many + negative = 40)
- Payment issues (none = 100, resolved = 80, ongoing = 40)
- Negative feedback (none = 100, some = 60, many = 20)

**Health Score Ranges:**
- **Green (80-100):** Healthy, expansion opportunity
- **Yellow (50-79):** At risk, needs attention
- **Red (0-49):** Critical, high churn risk

---

### Health Monitoring Dashboard

**Metrics Tracked:**
- Health score (current, trend)
- Usage metrics (logins, features, calls)
- Value metrics (ROI, revenue impact)
- Engagement metrics (support, training, feedback)
- Risk signals (tickets, payments, feedback)

**Alerts:**
- Health score drops below 50 (red alert)
- Health score drops below 80 (yellow alert)
- Support ticket spike (>3 tickets/week)
- Payment failure
- Negative feedback

**Actions:**
- Red alert: Immediate outreach (<24 hours)
- Yellow alert: Proactive check-in (<48 hours)
- Payment failure: Dunning sequence
- Negative feedback: Personal call from CSM
