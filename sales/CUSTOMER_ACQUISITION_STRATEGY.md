## Channel 1: Direct Outbound (Highest ROI)

### Strategy

**Target:** Auto shops using Tekmetric (8,000+ shops)

**Why This Works:**
- Already tech-forward (uses Tekmetric = open to AI)
- Warm list (we know they're qualified)
- Direct message (no intermediaries)
- Highest close rate (60% qualified leads)

### Execution Plan

**Step 1: Build Lead List**

**Sources:**
- Tekmetric customer directory (if available through partnership)
- Google Maps (search "auto repair [city]", filter for shops)
- Yelp / Google Business (filter by reviews, check if uses Tekmetric)
- Industry directories (ASA, ASE certified shops)
- LinkedIn (search "auto shop owner [city]")

**Data to Collect:**
- Shop name
- Owner name
- Phone number
- Email (if available)
- Location (city, state)
- Shop management system (Tekmetric preferred)
- Review count (indicator of size)

**List Size Target:**
- Week 1: 500 qualified leads (100 auto shops)
- Month 1: 2,000 qualified leads
- Month 6: 10,000 qualified leads

**Step 2: Multi-Touch Campaign**

**Touch 1: Email (Day 1)**
- Subject: "[Shop Name] - Are you missing $657K/year in after-hours calls?"
- Personalization: Shop name, owner name, local reference
- CTA: "15-minute call to see how Lake Street captured $657K/year"

**Touch 2: LinkedIn Connection (Day 2)**
- Personalized connection request
- Message: "Hi [Name], I help auto shops capture after-hours revenue. Saw [Shop Name] on Tekmetric. Worth a 15-min call?"

**Touch 3: Cold Call (Day 3)**
- Script: "Hi [Name], I'm [Your Name] with Cobalt AI. We help shops like yours capture after-hours calls. Quick question: How many after-hours calls are you missing per week?"
- Goal: Schedule discovery call or demo

**Touch 4: Follow-Up Email (Day 5)**
- Subject: "Following up - [Shop Name]"
- Reference previous touches
- Offer: "14-day trial, no cost"

**Step 3: Scale**

**Volume Targets:**
- Email: 100/day (personalized, sent manually or via tool)
- LinkedIn: 50/day (connection requests + messages)
- Cold calls: 30/day (high-value targets only)

**Resources:**
- 1 sales rep (founder): 30 calls/day
- Month 2: Add 1 SDR (Sales Development Rep): 50 emails + 30 LinkedIn/day
- Month 3: Add 1 AE (Account Executive): 20 calls/day (closes)

**Expected Results:**

| Month | Leads | Conversations | Demos | Customers | CAC |
|-------|-------|---------------|-------|-----------|-----|
| 1 | 500 | 250 | 150 | 100 | $500 |
| 2 | 1,000 | 500 | 300 | 180 | $400 |
| 3 | 1,500 | 750 | 450 | 270 | $350 |
| 4 | 2,000 | 1,000 | 600 | 360 | $300 |
| 5 | 2,500 | 1,250 | 750 | 450 | $275 |
| 6 | 3,000 | 1,500 | 900 | 540 | $250 |

**Total Month 1-6: 1,900 customers from direct outbound**

## Channel 2: Tekmetric Partnership (Co-Marketing)

### Strategy

**Approach:** Co-marketing partnership with Tekmetric

**Why This Works:**
- Their incentive: Make customers more successful (reduce churn)
- Our incentive: Access to 8,000 warm leads
- Joint value prop: "Tekmetric + Cobalt AI = Complete Solution"
- Higher close rate (40% vs 60% - colder leads, but higher volume)

### Partnership Structure

**Co-Marketing Agreement:**
- Tekmetric promotes Cobalt AI to their customers
- We promote Tekmetric to our customers (if they don't use it)
- Shared marketing materials (co-branded)
- Joint webinars and events
- Revenue sharing (optional - could be free promotion for them)

**Value Propositions:**

**For Tekmetric:**
- "Make your customers more successful"
- "Reduce customer churn" (successful customers stay)
- "Complete solution" (Tekmetric + AI = full stack)
- "Competitive differentiation" (only shop management system with AI)

**For Cobalt AI:**
- Access to 8,000 qualified leads
- Trusted brand association (Tekmetric endorsement)
- Lower CAC (partnership = free promotion)
- Higher volume (can't match with direct outbound alone)

### Execution Plan

**Phase 1: Relationship Building (Month 1)**
- Initial outreach to Tekmetric partnership team
- Demo Cobalt AI for Tekmetric leadership
- Present case study (Lake Street results)
- Propose co-marketing agreement

**Phase 2: Co-Marketing Launch (Month 2)**

**Webinar Series:**
- Monthly joint webinar: "How to Capture After-Hours Revenue"
- Tekmetric invites customers (email to 8,000 shops)
- We present Cobalt AI solution
- Q&A, demo, offer
- Expected: 200-300 attendees per webinar → 60-90 customers

**Email Campaign:**
- Co-branded email from Tekmetric to customers
- Subject: "New Integration: Capture After-Hours Revenue with AI"
- Content: Case study, demo link, special offer
- Expected: 5% open rate → 400 opens → 100 conversations → 40 customers

**Integration Marketplace:**
- List Cobalt AI in Tekmetric integration marketplace
- Featured integration (if partnership strong)
- Direct link from Tekmetric dashboard
- Expected: 50-100 sign-ups/month → 20-40 customers

**In-App Promotion:**
- Banner in Tekmetric dashboard (if partnership strong)
- "Try Cobalt AI - Capture After-Hours Revenue"
- Click → Demo → Customer
- Expected: 200 clicks/month → 80 customers

**Phase 3: Scale (Month 3-6)**

**Ongoing Activities:**
- Monthly webinars
- Quarterly email campaigns
- Integration marketplace listing
- Case studies (Tekmetric customers using Cobalt AI)
- Joint trade show presence

**Expected Results:**

## Channel 6: Referral Program

### Strategy

**Incentive Structure:**
- Referrer gets: 1 month free per referral (up to 12 months free)
- Referee gets: Founding 100 pricing ($49.95/month locked forever)
- Both win: Referrer saves money, referee gets discount

**Referral Process:**
1. Customer refers shop (email or link)
2. Referee signs up (mentions referrer)
3. Both get benefits (applied automatically)
4. Track referrals (dashboard shows referral count)

### Execution Plan

**Launch: Month 2 (After 100 customers)**

**Promotion:**
- Email to all customers: "Refer 3 Shops, Get 3 Months Free"
- In-app banner: "Know another shop? Refer them and save"
- Customer dashboard: Referral tracking widget

**Expected Referral Rate:**
- 20% of customers refer someone (industry average)
- Each referrer brings 1-2 shops (average 1.5)
- Month 2: 100 customers × 20% × 1.5 = 30 referrals
- Month 3: 200 customers × 20% × 1.5 = 60 referrals
- Month 4: 350 customers × 20% × 1.5 = 105 referrals
- Month 5: 550 customers × 20% × 1.5 = 165 referrals
- Month 6: 800 customers × 20% × 1.5 = 240 referrals

**Expected Results:**

| Month | Customers | Referrals | New Customers | CAC |
|-------|-----------|-----------|---------------|-----|
| 1 | 0 | 0 | 0 | - |
| 2 | 100 | 30 | 25 | $0 |
| 3 | 200 | 60 | 50 | $0 |
| 4 | 350 | 105 | 88 | $0 |
