## Role Definitions

### SDR (Sales Development Representative)

**Purpose:** Generate qualified leads and schedule demos

**Responsibilities:**
- Outbound prospecting (email, LinkedIn, cold calls)
- Lead qualification (Tekmetric user, 10+ calls/day, $300+ ticket)
- Demo scheduling (book 20 demos/week)
- Lead nurturing (follow-up sequences)
- CRM management (track all activities)

**Targets:**
- Demos scheduled: 20/week (80/month)
- Conversion rate: 50% (demo → qualified lead)
- Qualified leads: 40/month

**Skills Required:**
- Outbound sales experience (1-2 years)
- Excellent communication (written + verbal)
- Persistence (handles rejection)
- Organized (CRM, follow-ups, tracking)
- Technical aptitude (understand integrations, systems)

**Compensation:**
- Base: $50K/year
- Commission: $100/demo scheduled (qualified)
- OTE: $65K/year (30 qualified demos/month)

---

### AE (Account Executive)

**Purpose:** Close deals and hit revenue targets

**Responsibilities:**
- Demo execution (15-minute demos)
- Discovery calls (30-minute qualification)
- Proposal creation (custom proposals)
- Objection handling (all objections)
- Closing deals (60% close rate target)
- Account management (post-sale handoff)

**Targets:**
- Demos: 20/week (80/month)
- Close rate: 60% (qualified leads)
- Customers closed: 48/month
- ARR generated: $57K/month ($684K/year)

**Skills Required:**
- Closing experience (2-3 years)
- Consultative selling (discovery, value-based)
- Technical understanding (can explain integrations)
- Objection handling (experienced)
- Customer-centric (builds relationships)

**Compensation:**
- Base: $70K/year
- Commission: 10% of first-year ARR
- OTE: $138K/year (48 customers/month × $1,188 ARR × 10% = $5,702/month = $68K/year commission + $70K base)

---

### Sales Manager

**Purpose:** Manage and scale sales team

**Responsibilities:**
- Team management (SDRs, AEs)
- Training and coaching
- Process optimization
- Pipeline management
- Hiring and onboarding
- Performance tracking

**Targets:**
- Team quota: 200 customers/month (by Month 6)
- Team close rate: 55%+ (average)
- Team ARR: $238K/month ($2.85M/year)

**Skills Required:**
- Sales management (3-5 years)
- Hiring experience
- Training and coaching
- Process design
- Analytics and reporting

**Compensation:**
- Base: $100K/year
- Commission: 2% of team ARR
- OTE: $157K/year ($2.85M/year × 2% = $57K commission + $100K base)

---

### Sales Ops (Month 7+)

**Purpose:** Optimize sales processes, tools, and analytics

**Responsibilities:**
- CRM administration (HubSpot, Salesforce)
- Sales process optimization
- Analytics and reporting
- Tool management (email, LinkedIn, calling)
- Forecasting and pipeline analysis
- Sales enablement (materials, scripts, training)

**Skills Required:**
- Sales operations (2-3 years)
- CRM expertise (HubSpot preferred)
- Analytics (Excel, SQL, reporting)
- Process optimization
- Tool expertise (sales stack)

**Compensation:**
- Base: $80K/year
- Bonus: 10% of base ($8K/year) based on team performance
- OTE: $88K/year

**Day 6-7: Role-Playing**

## Compensation Structure

### SDR Compensation

**Base Salary:** $50K/year

**Commission:**
- $100 per qualified demo scheduled
- Qualified = meets all criteria (Tekmetric user, 10+ calls/day, $300+ ticket, decision maker)
- Capped at $15K/year commission (150 demos/year = 12.5/month average)

**OTE:** $65K/year
- Base: $50K
- Commission: $15K (12.5 demos/month average)

**Equity:** 0.05% - 0.1% (based on experience and performance)

---

### AE Compensation

**Base Salary:** $70K/year

**Commission:**
- 10% of first-year ARR per customer
- Example: $99/month customer = $1,188/year ARR × 10% = $118.80 commission
- No cap (top performers can earn unlimited)

**OTE:** $138K/year (at quota)
- Base: $70K
- Commission: $68K/year (48 customers/month × $1,188 ARR × 10% = $5,702/month = $68K/year)

**Quota:**
- 48 customers/month (1,188 ARR each = $57K ARR/month)
- 60% close rate (80 qualified leads → 48 customers)

**Equity:** 0.1% - 0.2% (based on experience and performance)

---

### Sales Manager Compensation

**Base Salary:** $100K/year

**Commission:**
- 2% of team ARR
- Team quota: $238K ARR/month ($2.85M/year)
- Commission: $2.85M × 2% = $57K/year

**OTE:** $157K/year
- Base: $100K
- Commission: $57K

**Equity:** 0.2% - 0.5% (based on team size and performance)

---

### Sales Ops Compensation

**Base Salary:** $80K/year

**Bonus:**
- 10% of base ($8K/year) based on team performance
- Team hits quota = full bonus
- Team exceeds quota = 125% bonus
- Team misses quota = prorated bonus

**OTE:** $88K/year

**Equity:** 0.05% - 0.1%
