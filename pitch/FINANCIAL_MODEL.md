## EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

This financial model projects Cobalt AI's growth from 200 locations (Year 1) to 25,000 locations (Year 5), achieving $30M ARR with 35% EBITDA margins. The model assumes:
- Capital-efficient growth model ($950/year infrastructure per vertical vs $1.35M traditional)
- Strong unit economics (70-85% gross margins at scale)
- Path to profitability by Month 18 (Year 2)
- Series A raise at Month 6 ($3M-5M at $40M-60M post)

## 3. UNIT ECONOMICS

### 3.1 LTV Calculation

**Formula:** `LTV = (ARPU × Gross Margin %) / Churn Rate`

**Example (Year 2, Standard Tier):**
- ARPU: $99/month = $1,188/year
- Gross Margin: 75%
- Annual Churn: 16%
- **LTV = ($1,188 × 0.75) / 0.16 = $5,568**

**LTV by Cohort:**
- Year 1 (3% monthly churn): $1,485 LTV
- Year 2 (1.5% monthly churn): $5,568 LTV
- Year 3+ (1% monthly churn): $8,910 LTV

### 3.2 CAC Calculation

**CAC Evolution:**
- Year 1: $500 CAC (founder-led, high-touch)
- Year 2: $400 CAC (sales team scaling)
- Year 3+: $300 CAC (product-market fit, referrals)

### 3.3 LTV:CAC Ratio

| Period | LTV | CAC | LTV:CAC | Target |
|--------|-----|-----|---------|--------|
| Year 1 | $1,485 | $500 | 3.0:1 | 3:1+ ✅ |
| Year 2 | $5,568 | $400 | 13.9:1 | 3:1+ ✅ |
| Year 3+ | $8,910 | $300 | 29.7:1 | 3:1+ ✅ |

**Target:** 3:1 minimum, improving to 10:1+ at scale

### 3.4 Gross Margin Evolution

**Formula:** `Gross Margin % = (Revenue - COGS) / Revenue`

**COGS Components:**
- AI/LLM costs (per location)
- Infrastructure costs (allocated per location)

| Period | Revenue/Location | AI Cost | Infrastructure | COGS | Gross Margin % |
|--------|------------------|---------|----------------|------|----------------|
| Year 1 | $99 | $100 | $2.45 | $102.45 | -3.5% (early stage) |
| Year 2 Q1 | $99 | $80 | $0.60 | $80.60 | 18.6% |
| Year 2 Q4 | $99 | $67 | $0.60 | $67.60 | 31.7% |
| Year 3 | $99 | $67 | $0.40 | $67.40 | 31.9% |
| Year 4+ | $99 | $67 | $0.30 | $67.30 | 32.0% |

**Premium Tier Impact:**
- Premium ($199/month): Gross Margin 67%+ (higher revenue, same costs)

**At Scale (1,000+ locations, premium mix):**
- **Gross Margin: 70-85%** (premium tier, optimized costs)

### 3.5 Contribution Margin

**Formula:** `Contribution Margin = Revenue - Variable Costs (AI + Infrastructure)`

| Period | Revenue/Location | Variable Costs | Contribution Margin | Contribution % |
|--------|------------------|----------------|---------------------|----------------|
| Year 1 | $99 | $102.45 | -$3.45 | -3.5% |
| Year 2 | $99 | $67.60 | $31.40 | 31.7% |
| Year 3+ | $99 | $67.30 | $31.70 | 32.0% |

**Note:** Year 1 negative contribution margin is expected (optimization phase, founding pricing)
